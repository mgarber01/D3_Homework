Correlations Discovered Between Income,Smoking and Obesity.

The Visualiztion above displays the correlation between Mean Income and Obesity, as well as Mean Income and Smoking by state.

Each bubble on the chart represents a U.S. State (hover over to see which one!).

The chart clearly demonstrates that in the case of both health indicators, Smoking and Obesity, citizens in wealthier States tend toward less of either. Studies are underway to determine the root causes of this phenomenon.
